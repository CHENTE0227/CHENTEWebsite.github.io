Hello, I am Vicente Huffington, and I am excited to present my practice art website. This mini-site serves as a platform to showcase a collection of my artwork and provide a glimpse into how they would appear if made available for purchase. Most of these drawings are doodles and are not anything too polished.
Enjoy!

Utilizing Bootstrap, HTML, and CSS I made this site. I always thought it'd be handy to learn web design/development as most businesses or sellers use the web to display their products or works. So this is what I made, hoping that I will only get better from here and utilize the tools available in today's age to advertise myself as an artist.
